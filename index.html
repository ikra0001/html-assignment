About Me

I am a student in Algonquin College, I enjoy 3D printing in different formats but prefer MSLA printing. I like the creative digital arts, be it motion graphics, graphic design or animation I dabble in everything. I finished a year and a half of Graphic Design, but it felt too focused compared to what I wanted. The Interactive Media Design program so far covers more subjects with a little more detail, and I'm glad I transfered into this program.

I lived in Canada for 9 years now, moved around a lot changing different colleges and cities, but now that I'm in ottawa it is a nice to finally settle down and not worry about the next move. I have been managing a few properties my parents own and have been working on renovating them, to work our way up for higher value real estate. Been a hard time continuing my studies in between, but I do my best.
